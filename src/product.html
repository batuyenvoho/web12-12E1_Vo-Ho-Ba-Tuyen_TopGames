ABOUT THIS GAME

Elden Ring is an action RPG developed by FromSoftware and published by Bandai Namco Entertainment, created in collaboration with fantasy author George R. R. Martin. Set in the mystical Lands Between, players take on the role of a Tarnished — an exiled warrior summoned back to claim the shattered fragments of the Elden Ring and become Elden Lord.

Explore a breathtaking open world filled with dungeons, legacy castles, and deadly bosses. The game blends the brutal challenge of the Soulsborne series with open-world freedom that has never been seen before in the genre.

KEY FEATURES

  • Massive open world — the Lands Between
  • Story co-written with George R. R. Martin
  • 100+ unique bosses & deep combat system
  • Online co-op & PvP multiplayer
  • Fully customisable character builds
  • Rich lore hidden across every corner of the map
